Classical

Classical music is a genre that has had a profound impact on our society and culture, with a rich history spanning from medieval and renaissance music to the modern era.

Rock

Rock genre of music emerged in the 1950s and can be defined as “a form of music with a strong beat”—it is difficult to be much more precise. It is also called rock and roll.

Hip-Hop

Hip Hip-Hop is a genre of music most often characterized by a strong, rhythmic beat and a rapping vocal track.

Dance

Electronic dance music (EDM), also referred to as dance music, is a broad range of percussive electronic music genres originally made for nightclubs, raves, and festivals.
